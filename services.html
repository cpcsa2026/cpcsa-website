Our services

Cleaning, lawn, garden and property care services.

Complete Property Care SA is built to help customers manage the inside and outside of their property through one reliable local team.

01

Home Care

Practical residential cleaning support for busy households, families, tenants, landlords and property owners who want their home kept clean and presentable.

  • Regular residential cleaning
  • Kitchen and bathroom cleaning
  • Dusting, vacuuming and mopping
  • General tidy and presentation cleaning
  • One-off cleans and scheduled recurring cleans
02

Lawn & Garden

Ongoing lawn and garden maintenance designed to keep outdoor areas tidy, healthy and under control throughout the year.

  • Lawn mowing and edging
  • Regular lawn maintenance
  • Garden maintenance and green waste tidy-ups
  • Weed monitoring and seasonal fertilising
  • Yearly lawn renewal, including scarifying and aerating where suitable
03

Exterior Property Care

Exterior presentation services for driveways, paths, outdoor areas and general property appearance.

  • Pressure washing
  • Driveway and path cleaning
  • Outdoor area tidy-ups
  • Exterior surface presentation
  • Pre-inspection or pre-sale property clean-up support
04

Property Management Services

Support services for landlords, busy property owners and short-stay property operators who need reliable presentation and routine care.

  • Property Health Checks
  • Routine property presentation checks
  • Landlord and property owner support
  • Airbnb-ready cleaning and exterior presentation support
  • Scheduled care packages for ongoing maintenance
05

Irrigation & Reticulation Repairs and Maintenance

Inspection, maintenance and basic repair support for residential irrigation and reticulation systems, helping lawns and gardens receive the right water at the right time.

  • Sprinkler checks and basic repairs
  • Drip irrigation inspection and servicing
  • Controller programming
  • Seasonal watering optimisation
  • Leak identification and maintenance advice
  • Licensed plumbing work excluded unless arranged separately
